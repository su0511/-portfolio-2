Susan He

is pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design at Rhode Island School of Design.

She likes typography that breathes, colors with restraint, and layouts that feel just right. Her work aims to be clear, calm, and quietly engaging — designed with attention, not noise. She also loves dogs, slow walks, and making things that invite a second look.
